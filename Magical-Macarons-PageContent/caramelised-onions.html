🧅🕑Caramelised Onions!🕑🧅

To make this bitter and sweet, enhancer of meals, you will need...!

🍴 Utensils you will need:✨

  • A small pot
  • A wooden spoon
  • A chopping board and big knife 🔪

Ingredients Necessary: ✨

  • Make these check boxes with strike through
  • A small / medium whole onion 🧅
  • A couple pinches of salt 🧂
  • vegetable oil

      Caramelisers 🍯🔥

    • brown sugar
    • vinegar
    • balsamic vinegar

Food Prep!✨

This should be done before the method.

To ease the process.
  • 🧅 WASH YOUR ONION, THEY'RE FROM THE GROUND!!🧅

Let's get to the Method!✨

  1. First, chop the ends of your onion. 🧅
  2. Next, we are ready to slice them by cutting along the onion vertically slicing thinly.
  3. Once the onion has been sliced they are ready to go in the pot & on the hob.✨
  4. add all of the ingredients together in the pot and stir with a wooden spoon.
  5. They can sit on a low-medium heat or 2 on a hob, they need to be frequently stirred across 40mins.🕑❤
  6. They should look golden, soggy and a lot more broken down now, so we turn up the heat briefly to a 3 and back down to a 2, to add a bit of char to your caramelised onions!🔥
  7. This then needs to cool down a bit before adding to another recipe, such as Sausage Rolls OR Meat Pies OR (for the veggies)Cheese and Onion Pasties OR Chicken & Bacon Pasties

    8. This should make enough onions to use for another recipe!

Voila! Bon Appetit!✨🧅
