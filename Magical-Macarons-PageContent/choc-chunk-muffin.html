🧁🍫Choc Chunk Muffins!🍫🧁

To make these sweet treats, you will need...!

The Utensils you will need:✨

  • An electric hand mixer OR alternatively some elbow grease and a wooden spoon!
  • A hand whisk
  • 1 Metal cooking trays
  • A large plastic bowl
  • Various pieces of cutlery
  • A chopping board and big knife
  • Weighing Scales & measuring cups
  • Sieve
  • Silicon cupcake moulds OR cupcake cases

Ingredients Necessary: ✨

  • Make these check boxes with strike through
  • 80g unsalted butter 🧈
  • 75g Castor sugar
  • 75g Brown sugar
  • 150g of milk chocolate cooking choc ( you may substitute dark chocolate for a more refined taste, but I prefer a sweeter muffins🍫🍫🍫)
  • 180g Plain flour
  • One large egg 🥚
  • a few drops of Vanilla Flavouring or Extract
  • OPTIONAL:few drops red food colouring / gel will make the cookies RED VELVET <--LINK THAT TO AN IMAGE idk
  • 1 1/2 tbsp bicarbonate of soda
  • A pinch of salt

Food Prep!🍫

This should be done before the method.

To ease the process.
  • get the butter out beforehand so it can warm up to room temperature, it will be easier to work with.
  • chop the block of chocolate into chunks.
  • Grease your baking tray / or lay down some baking paper so nothing sticks.
  • lay out your cupcake cases, if you are using those ; if you're using silicon moulds either grease them or spray them with some grease.
  • set the oven to fan 180 degrees.

Let's get to the Method!✨

  1. First, sift in your dry ingrdients into large mixing bowl and whisk them together manually (if you use the electric mixer at this stage, you will look like Micheal Jackson).
  2. Next, add the egg and butter, combine together to form a cake batter.(you can use the electric mixer at this stage 😊).
  3. Once our cake batter has been formed, you can chuck in your chunked chocolate. (optionally add food colouring at this stage).
  4. We now have, a choc-chunk cake batter, that we can start scooping into our cupcake cases/moulds.❤
  5. fill the cases/moulds between 1/2 and 3/4 of the way up, so that thee muffins have room to grow in the oven (the bicarb helps them grow!).
  6. place in oven and cook for 20-25 minutes or until golden (Tip : Check with a toothpick or knife as to whether they have cooked knife test).
  7. I use quite big moulds and so made 6 cupcakes, though with smaller cases i think you could get more smaller muffins, serves 6-8 choc chunk cupcakes.
  8. Allow to cool down on the side for 5-10 minutes, so that they can firm up.

Voila! Bon Appetit!✨🍪
