🍟🥔Homemade Chips!🥔🍟

To make this savoury treat, you will need...!

🍴 Utensils you will need:✨

  • A metal cooking tray
  • A large plastic bowl
  • A chopping board and big knife 🔪

Ingredients Necessary: ✨

  • Make these check boxes with strike through
  • A few potatoes 🥔🥔🥔🥔
  • A couple pinches of salt 🧂
  • 1 litre of Water 🌊
  • vegetable oil

Food Prep!✨🍟🧂

This should be done before the method.

To ease the process.
  • 🥔 WASH YOUR POTATOES , THEY'RE FROM THE GROUND!!🥔
  • set the oven to fan 180 degrees.🔥
  • Grease your pan if you're using the OVEN method of cooking

Let's get to the Method!✨

  1. First, chop the ends of your potatoes.
  2. Next, chop all the sides down until there is no skin left on the potatoes.
  3. Once the potatoes flesh is revealed, we are ready to slice them into chips by cutting along the potato vertically, at the desired length of the person preparing them, i personally cut them 1.5cm thick approx.
  4. We now have, potato slices, the slices cut from the middle may be a bit too long, i recommend cutting them in half vertically so that all the chips are similar sizes and cook through at the same time.❤
  5. You may think that we're done here, but I assure we are not, Theres still one magic step to making chips!
  6. Place all of the potato slices into a mixing bowl and add cold water until they're submerged, add a few pinches of salt and let rest for an hour and a half, at room temperature.
  7. Once the chips have rested, they are ready to be drained and cooked up, there are two methods in which you can cook chips
  8. OVEN: place in oven and cook for 30-35 minutes or until golden and crisp.
  9. DEEPFAT FRIER: place the dial on between 1-2 o'clock. 🕐🕑 whilst the oil is heating up , submerge the chips in the warm oil for a few seconds then place them on the stand again.(this is called blanching and the key to the perfect fried chips)🍟
  10. Then when the oil is ready submerge the chips again, this time waiting until the chips rise. (TIP:They are ready when you see them all floating in the oil).
  11. leave to stand for a few minutes before eating and season to your preference.🧂salt, pepper and vinegar for me!
  12. NOTE:AIR FRIERS are a thing , i just don't have one so can't comment on how to use it to prepare chips

    13. This should make 2 portions of chips! So feel free to share this recipe!

Voila! Bon Appetit!✨🍟
