🍪🍫Choc Chunk Cookies!🍫🍪

To make these sweet treats, you will need...!

The Utensils you will need:✨

  • An electric hand mixer / or some elbow grease and a wooden spoon!
  • 2 metal cooking trays
  • a plastic bowl
  • various pieces of cutlery
  • A chopping board and big knife
  • Weighing Scales & measuring cups
  • Sieve

Ingredients Necessary: ✨

  • Make these check boxes with strike through
  • 125g unsalted butter 🧈
  • Half a cup of Icing sugar
  • 75g Castor sugar
  • 75g Brown sugar
  • 150g of DARK cooking chocolate (I use too much sometimes, but that's really not good nutritonally speaking🍫🍫🍫)
  • Plain flour
  • One medium egg 🥚
  • a few drops of Vanilla Flavouring or Extract
  • OPTIONAL:few drops red food colouring / gel will make the cookies RED VELVET <--LINK THAT TO AN IMAGE idk
  • 1/2 tbsp bicarbonate of soda

Food Prep!🍫

This should be done before the method.

To ease the process.
  • get the butter out beforehand so it can warm up to room temperature, it will be easier to work with.
  • chop the block of chocolate into chunks.
  • beat(mix it up with a fork) the egg in a bowl.
  • Grease your baking trays.
  • set the oven to fan 180 degrees.

Let's get to the Method!✨

  1. First cream the butter and sugar toegether in a medium to large mixing bowl.
  2. Next, sift in your dry ingrdients and add the beaten egg combine together to form a dough.
  3. Once a dough like blob has been formed, you can chuck in your chunked chocolate
  4. You now have a choc-chunk cookie dough (optionally add food colouring at this stage)
  5. fill a cup with icing sugar and roll out little balls, about an inch wide, from your dough and drop them in the cup ensuring to coat the ball evenly.
  6. place the balls on your trays, i keep to 6 cookies a tray, and this recipe should make 10-12 cookies.
  7. place in oven and cook for 10-12 minutes or until golden.
  8. Allow to cool down on the side for 5-10 minutes so that they can firm up.

Voila! Bon Appetit!✨🍪
