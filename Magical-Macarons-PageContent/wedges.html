🔥🍟🥔Homemade (Spicy) Wedges!🥔🍟🔥

To make this savoury & spicy treat, you will need...!

🍴 Utensils you will need:✨

  • A metal cooking tray
  • A large plastic bowl
  • A chopping board and big knife 🔪

Ingredients Necessary: ✨

  • Make these check boxes with strike through
  • A few potatoes 🥔🥔🥔🥔
  • A couple pinches of salt 🧂
  • vegetable oil
  • 1 litre of Water 🌊

      Spices & Seaonings 🔥🌶🍟

    • Parika
    • american bbq seasoning
    • chili powder
    • chili flakes
    • cayenne pepper powder
    • garlic granules
    • general seasoning

Food Prep!✨🍟🧂🌶

This should be done before the method.

To ease the process.
  • 🥔 WASH YOUR POTATOES , THEY'RE FROM THE GROUND!!🥔
  • set the oven to fan 180 degrees.🔥

Let's get to the Method!✨

  1. First, chop the ends of your potatoes.(and any roots if you've got them)
  2. Next, we are ready to slice them into wedges by cutting along the potatoes vertically slicing approx 1.5cm in thickness.
  3. Once the potato has been sliced it will need slicing again on the other side to create the perfect wedges.✨
  4. We now have, wedge like slices, the slices cut from the middle may be a bit too long, cut them in half vertically so that all the wedges are similar sizes and cook through at the same time.❤
  5. Now we need to let these wedges marinate in some water to soften them up a bit!
  6. Place all of the potato slices into a mixing bowl and add cold water until they're submerged, add a few pinches of salt and let rest for an hour and a half, at room temperature.
  7. Once the wedges have rested, they are ready to be drained.
  8. When the wedges have been drained, they are ready for seasoning , add a few teaspoons of vegetable oil into the mixing bowl the wedges are in.
  9. This will help the spices stick to our wedges, get out a fork to spin and toss your wedges around in the bowl.
  10. Adding spices is up to you , i personally love a kick to my wedges, this is how i season :
  11. 3 tablespoons of paprika, a teaspoon of chili powder, cayenne pepper powder, and chili flakes.Stir around with a fork (if you let this sit the flavour will seep into the wedges very coresss)
  12. Seasoning : 2 tablespoons of american bbq seasoning , 1 tablespoon general purpose seasoning and half a teaspoon of garlic granules
  13. spin and flip the wedges inside of the bowl , with a fork, ensuring to coat each wedge sufficiently.
  14. Grease your tray, before putting the wedges on it and shake the tray a little to level out the wedges.
  15. OVEN: place in oven and cook for 30-35 minutes or until browned and crisp.

    16. This should make 2 portions of wedges! So feel free to share this recipe! Or fridge it and keep it to yourself!

Voila! Bon Appetit!✨🍟🌶
