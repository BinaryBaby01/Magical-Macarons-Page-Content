Meat / Pork Pies (You can substitute your prefered meat!)

  • Various pieces of cutlery

  • Large mixing bowl

Ingredients Necessary: ‚ú®

  • Make these check boxes with strike through
  • 50g plain flour (for rolling out pastry)
  • 400ml boiling water
  • Chicken | Ham | Veg stock cube
  • Gelatine (or another gelling agent)
  • 500g Shortcrust-Pustry or make your own Shortcrust Pastry
  • One small egg ü•ö (for egg wash)
  • a splash of water
  • pepper
  • A pinch of salt
  • One small onion (Caramelised Onions go great in this recipe!)
  • 20g Grated Mozzarella
  • 8 Sausage Skinless (or remove the skins manually)

Food Prep!üßÖ

This should be done before the method.

To ease the process.
  • Get the butter out beforehand so it can warm up to room temperature, it will be easier to work with.
  • Chop the onion into thin slices.
  • Grease your baking tray / or lay down some baking paper so nothing sticks.
  • Beat the egg and add a splash of water in a bowl, this is the pastry glaze.
  • set the oven to fan 180 degrees.

Let's get to the Method!‚ú®

  1. Firstly, put all the skinless sausages into a bowl.
  2. Secondly, you can either use Caramelised Onions or just add you chopped raw onions to the sausage mix and it will cook when they go into the oven. üòä.
  3. Then, add the mozzarella to the mix and mix the ingredients together with your fingers until you have combined the ingredients into one big ball of mushed meat, feel free to season with salt & pepper..
  4. Once we have incorporated all of the mozzarella, we can wash our hands and move to the next step, ensuring to cover to keep the meat moist! ‚ù§
  5. We now have our meat pie filling, now we move on to the pastry, coat your side in flour so that we can roll out our pastry. Shortcrust Pastry
  6. cut a quarter off of the pastry, this is to be saved for making the lids for the pies, now we have our pastry roll it out to an ¬£1 thickness or approx. 1cm thick and cut into 4 pieces.
  7. Then, take the jar / cup / moulds, and wrap your pastry around the bottom of the jar or cup to make a pie base, or fit it into your moulds.
  8. Now we need to fill our pie bases with the meat filling we prepared before, use spoons to fill the pies up.
  9. Quickly make some pie lids, roll out the remaining pastry and cut into 4, use a straw to poke a hole into the top of each one.
  10. Apply the pie lids to the meat bowls, and then take any corner pastry off before sealing the pie by pinching and folding the corners of the pastry together.
  11. put the moulds or pies in the tray.
  12. Place in the oven and allow to cook for 50 mins
  13. We also need to make a veg stock of 400ml and add gelatine and stir until it is all dissolved.
  14. Once the pies have cooked, we can pour in the gelatinous stock mix, into the holes.
  15. Allow to cool down on the side for 15-20 minutes, or until room temperature and then refridgerate for atleast 4 hours to let the jelly set.
