Food Prep!üßÖ

This should be done before the method.

To ease the process.
  • Get the butter out beforehand so it can warm up to room temperature, it will be easier to work with.
  • Chop the onion into thin slices.
  • Grease your baking tray / or lay down some baking paper so nothing sticks.

Let's get to the Method!‚ú®

  1. Firstly, melt the butter in a pan, on the hob on a low heat, so we don't burn the butter.
  2. Secondly, add the milk and flour and stir frequently with the wooden spoon. üòä.
  3. Then, turn the heat up to a medium and add the mozzarella cheese first , a bit at a time, stirring frequently still, it may look lumpy rn but it will al come together.
  4. Once we have incorporated all of the mozzarella,we can add the cheddar to the mix! ‚ù§
  5. We now have our cheese sauce, feel free to season with salt & pepper.
  6. the cheese sauce, now needs the addition of the onions to be cheese & onion, and OPTIONALLY mushrooms or leeks! stir and let simmer for a few minutes on medium-low heat.
  7. Then, take the cheese sauce, pasty filling and turn off the hob and allow it to cool on the hob.
  8. Open the pastry , and shake plain flour onto your side (Tip : or make your own Puff Pastry.
  9. place in oven and cook for 20-25 minutes or until golden (Tip : or add a different filling Chicken & Bacon Pasties.
  10. Allow to cool down on the side for 5-10 minutes, so that they can firm up.
  11. Serves: 2-3 veggie pasties.
